Math Story about Seven Fingers People (1)

Some where on earth, there is a kingdom. All the people live there only has seven fingers.

For a long time, 7 fingers people are using the same math as we 10 fingers people learned. But one day, the king of seven fingers kingdom find it is not easy for his people to do the counting as 10 fingers people doing. So he decided to ask Eric's help to create a new math textbook for their kids - <The Mathematics for Seven-Fingers People>.

Eric is very glad to do that. So he take it as a challenge.
