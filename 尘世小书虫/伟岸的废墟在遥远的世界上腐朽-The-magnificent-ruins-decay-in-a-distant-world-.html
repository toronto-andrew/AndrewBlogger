伟岸的废墟在遥远的世界上腐朽 The magnificent ruins decay in a distant world.

As time passes, we experience various changes and witness the rise and fall of many things that were once considered magnificent. These things were seen as indispensable during their glorious periods but gradually abandoned and turned into ruins over time.

This phenomenon is not limited to the material world but also includes our values and pursuits. Just as grand ruins decay, the values we once deemed crucial may be discarded by people today. It serves as a warning, reminding us to examine the present and contemplate whether what we pursue truly holds lasting significance.

The value of knowledge is shifting from scarcity to abundance, and symbols of status are increasingly deceptive. Even religious beliefs are undergoing changes. Christianity, for instance, has greatly diminished in influence in Western societies, and other forms of monotheism face various doubts and challenges. Religion is no longer viewed as the sole spiritual guidance and source of meaning in life, as science and rationality replace ignorance and superstition. Nationalism, once considered an important source of social cohesion, is now wavering as global integration diminishes national consciousness. Patriotism is sometimes viewed as an excuse or a means of manipulation rather than a firm belief.

In Chinese society 20-30 years ago, many people pursued material wealth and utilitarian success. Money, fame, and status became standards for measuring individual worth. However, do these pursuits truly bring happiness and fulfillment? Meanwhile, Western society during the same period realized that these goals were not as important and focused more on spiritual richness and genuine relationships.

Ancient values have provided us with many important principles and guidance. Traditional moral concepts, family values, and social responsibilities were considered supreme in ancient times. However, with the waves of modernization and globalization, many traditional values are seen as outdated and restrictive. Individualism and free will are gradually rising, as people increasingly pursue personal rights and self-fulfillment.

In the contemporary world, we see the emergence of new values. Issues such as sustainable development, multiculturalism, gender equality, and social justice receive increasing attention. People place more emphasis on environmental protection, social responsibility, and inclusivity. This shift in values reflects contemporary society's pursuit of sustainability and social justice.

However, future values may face new challenges and changes. With the continuous development of technology and the rise of artificial intelligence, we may need to reconsider the intrinsic value and meaning of humanity. Alongside advancements in biotechnology, ethical and moral questions will become more complex. We may need to redefine human dignity, privacy rights, and moral boundaries.

Additionally, we face challenges brought by globalization. Cross-cultural differences in values require us to adopt a more open and inclusive approach to different perspectives and beliefs. Future values may need to emphasize diversity, dialogue, and coexistence.

The metaphor of grand ruins decaying in a distant world reminds us that things once regarded as supreme may gradually lose their importance through the erosion of time. We should examine our own values and pursuits and reflect on what we truly cherish. Perhaps in the future, we will redefine the meanings of success, happiness, and progress, placing greater emphasis on spiritual richness, interpersonal relationships, and environmental protection.

All these changes represent a more rational human society that emphasizes individuality, independent thinking, and tolerance. People no longer rely solely on traditional authorities and dogmas but construct their own beliefs based on rational thought. This also causes some previously firm ideas and values to become shaky and distant "ruins" in memory. However, each generation nostalgically looks back and yearns for the past because it represents their youth and ideals. The thoughts and values that today's generation passionately pursues and values may face questioning and abandonment in the next generation. This is the inevitable path of social progress, but it also serves as a reminder for us to discover the eternal amidst the changes and not forget our emotional side amidst rationality. Maintaining an openand inclusive mind is the best way to face the changes of the times.

Therefore, let us continuously reflect and examine to avoid losing what is truly important in the pursuit, making grand ruins serve as our cautionary reminder.
